Our Story

Founded in 2008, Infinitax has grown from a small local practice to one of the most respected tax advisory firms in the UK. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide clear, expert tax advice that helps our clients keep more of what they earn.

Today, we serve hundreds of businesses and individuals across the UK, helping them navigate the complex world of taxation with confidence. Our team of chartered accountants brings together decades of experience in all aspects of UK tax law.
